Metrics

Metrics are measures of quantitative assessment.

What are Matrics

  • Matrics are used for assessing, comparing and tracking performances or production of company.Group of metrics will typically be used to review performance assessments.
  • With the help of performance matrics it is possible to compare growth of companies from two different industies.
  • Metrics quantify the companies growth, production and efficiency which are essential factors to be consider for any investor.
  • Popular Investor Metrics are:
  • Price-to-earning
  • Price-to-book
  • Price/earning to growth ratio
  • Debt-equity
  • Free-cashFlow
