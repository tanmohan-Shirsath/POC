Economic Moat

Economic Moat is the competive advatage that one company has over other companiesin the Same industry.

Understanding Economic Moat

  • Economic Moat has distinct advantages as it clearly distinct a company over it's competitors.
  • It is often an advantage that is difficult to mimic or duplicate (brand identity, patents) and thus creates an effective barrier against competition from other firms.
  • For marketable security of publicly treaded comapnies, It is required to issue periodic financial information to the public to need the full knowldge requirement.
  • Every successful company understands that the main threat to their continued success will be from competitors, and keeping them at bay is critical to sustaining their dominance.
  • This is important not only to the company's bottom line but also to potential investors seeking to maximize their portfolios by including companies that will maintain their performance edge.
  • The most obvious financial characteristics that companies with a wide economic moat have is that they usually generate large amounts of free cash flow and have a track record of strong returns.
